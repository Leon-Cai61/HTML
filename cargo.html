Cargo Aircraft

Cargo aircraft, similar to bomber aircraft, serve a specific purpose related to transportation. A cargo aircraft is designed and built to transport various types of cargo, such as goods, supplies, equipment, or even personnel. These aircraft are specifically configured to carry a large volume or heavy loads efficiently and safely over long distances.Cargo aircraft come in different sizes and configurations to accommodate different cargo requirements. They are typically equipped with spacious cargo holds, loading ramps, or large doors to facilitate the loading and unloading of goods. The interior of a cargo aircraft can be modified with cargo handling systems, such as pallets, containers, or rollers, to ensure secure and organized transport of diverse types of cargo.
